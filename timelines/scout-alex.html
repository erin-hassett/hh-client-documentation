Welcome

To Your Wedding Day Timeline

Hello Scout,

We're in the final stretch! Below is your timeline, built from everything you shared. I've mapped out exactly when things happen so you can relax and be present.

· · · · · · ·

Why This Timeline Matters

I used to wear a lot of hats on wedding days. Photographer, timeline keeper, family wrangler, DJ liaison. I was good at it. But every minute I spent tracking someone down was a minute I wasn't with you. And your day is full of moments that only happen once: the glance across the room when it hits you that you're married, your dad pretending he's totally fine and not crying at all. Those moments don't wait. If I'm solving a logistics problem, I miss them.

So I made a choice. I want to be your photographer, fully present and fully yours, not a coordinator with a camera.

· · · · · · ·

Who Owns The Timeline

Someone needs to run point on your wedding day, and I need it to not be me. You have Jaclyn from Veil Events, so you're in great hands. She knows the plan inside and out, and she'll run point so you and your people can just be present.

· · · · · · ·

The Tradeoff

The more your people know the plan and take ownership of it, the more I can do what you're actually paying me for. When something goes sideways, I'll flag it to Jaclyn. Then I stay with you. I'm not disappearing to track down your dad for a first look. I want to be right there when he sees you. That's the photo. That's what you'll frame.

Share This Timeline

Please share this with your wedding party, parents, and anyone else who needs to know the plan. The more seriously everyone takes this, the more incredible your album will be. I promise it's worth it.

I can't wait,
-Erin

Hair + Makeup Complete

This is a hard deadline

If hair and makeup runs late, everything compresses: dress time gets rushed, portraits get cut short, and I end up choosing between skipping moments or making you late for your own ceremony.

Why this matters

When everyone sticks to this timeline, you get breathing room. Time to have a snack, use the restroom, take a breath before the big transformation.
